## Background

- This is the v1.1.0 Stellar ITS release.

Contract changes in the release:

1. Use contractstorage macro on storage enum.

Deployment changes in the release:

1. Add 'stellar/its.js encode-recipient ' command to encode Stellar recipient addresses to bytes from the CLI.
2. Add 'stellar/its.js execute' command to execute ITS messages from the CLI.
3. Make gas-token-address and gas-fee-amount optional for 'stellar/its.js deploy-remote-interchain-token', 'stellar/its.js deploy-remote-canonical-token', and 'stellar/its.js interchain-transfer' commands.
4. Update 'stellar/deploy-contract.js' chain-name for ITS initialize args.
5. Remove original-chain from 'evm/interchainTokenFactory.js --action deployRemoteCanonicalInterchainToken' command.
